About Me

I am a research scholar at the Egypt-Japan University of Science and Technology, specializing in antenna design, reconfigurable intelligent surfaces, and wireless power transfer for high-mobility applications. With expertise in tools like MATLAB, Python, and CST Studio Suite, I have contributed to international journals and conferences. My research interests include advanced antenna systems, metasurface technologies, vehicular communications, physical layer security, and AI-driven wireless networks.
