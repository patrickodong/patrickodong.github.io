About Me

I am a research scholar at the Egypt-Japan University of Science and Technology, specializing in antenna design, reconfigurable intelligent surfaces, and wireless power transfer for high-mobility applications. With expertise in tools like MATLAB, Python, Mathematica, CST Studio Suite, Keysight ADS, and AWRDE, I have contributed to international journals and conferences. My research interests include advanced antenna design, radar systems, fluid antenna system, reconfigurable intelligent surfaces, non-reconfigurable metasurface coated devices, radio frequency wireless energy transfer, signal processing, vehicular communications, channel modeling, physical layer security, open radio access network, integrated sensing and communications, software defined radios, artificial intelligence, free space optics, intra-/inter-aircraft networks.

Research Topics

  1. Security and Reliability of Smart Agricultural Systems

    This topic discusses the importance of ensuring security and reliability in smart agricultural systems, focusing on the use of advanced technologies to improve the safety and efficiency of farming operations.

  2. Towards Secure and Reliable Intelligent Transportation System

    This topic explores the development of secure and reliable communication networks for intelligent transportation systems, aiming to enhance road safety, traffic management, and vehicle-to-vehicle communication.

  3. Antenna Design and Analysis for Radio Frequency Energy Harvesting Vehicular Networks

    This topic covers the design and analysis of antennas for energy harvesting in vehicular networks, focusing on the integration of radio frequency energy harvesting technologies to power wireless communication devices.

  4. A Secure Multi-User Selection Scheme for the Internet of Things

    This topic presents a secure multi-user selection scheme for the Internet of Things (IoT), ensuring secure communication and efficient resource allocation in a highly connected IoT environment.
